Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-03

This privacy policy applies to my personal smart home integration that connects Google Home/Google Assistant to my Home Assistant instance.

Data processed

  • Device metadata (names, types, traits) for exposed entities
  • Device state (e.g., on/off, temperature/humidity) for exposed entities
  • Request metadata needed to fulfill commands

How data is used

  • To fulfill Google Assistant requests (SYNC/QUERY/EXECUTE).
  • To keep device state updated (if state reporting is enabled).

Data sharing

Data is shared with Google only as required for Google Assistant smart home functionality. I do not sell personal data.

Data retention

Home Assistant retains data/logs according to my Home Assistant configuration. Google retains data according to my Google account and Google policies.

Security

Transport is protected by HTTPS (TLS) to my endpoint. Access is controlled via OAuth account linking.

Your choices

  • Unlink the service at any time in the Google Home app.
  • Stop exposing entities in Home Assistant at any time.
